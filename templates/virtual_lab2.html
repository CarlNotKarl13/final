Introducing our innovative modeling tool

Designed to optimize the composition and production of composite cements, this tool is built upon three powerful models, enabling you to:

  • Calculate the kinetics of cement hydration, offering insights into the curing process.
  • Monitor the evolution of the phase assemblage, including apparent porosity, which is crucial for assessing cement quality.
  • Predict the development of compressive strength while also evaluating the environmental impact of cement production, all based on the chosen cement composition and fineness of its components.
